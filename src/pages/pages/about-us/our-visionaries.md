# Our Visionaries


## MR. VIVEK BHARAT RAM


Mr. Vivek Bharat Ram, the youngest of the three sons of Dr. Bharat Ram, holds a B.S. Industrial Engineering degree from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, Michigan, USA. He became the Chairman and Senior Managing Director of DCM Benetton India Ltd and Chairman of Shriram Centennial Enterprises Ltd. Currently he is the Chairman of Shri Ram New Horizons Limited and Shri Ram Education Trust (SRET).
