# Assessment


A regular assessment of students work is considered crucial in the educational process. Progress would be discussed with both parents and students. In Pre-Primary, school the assessment will be based on class work, homework, projects, and short assisted quizzes and worksheets.


Report frequency -Students will receive three reports in a year. Reports include an objective assessment of the effort and achievement of the student as well as personalized comments by teachers.
