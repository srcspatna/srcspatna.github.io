## Steps for a Smooth Transition When Switching Schools in Patna

Preparation is the key to minimizing stress for your child.

### 1. Administrative Requirements
When **switching schools in Patna**, the most critical document is the **Transfer Certificate (TC)** from the previous school. For inter-state or inter-board transfers, ensure the TC is countersigned by the appropriate board authority (CBSE/ICSE/State Board).

### 2. Assessing Academic Alignment
Every school has a slightly different pace. When **switching to SRCS Patna**, we conduct a friendly placement assessment to understand your child's current level, ensuring we place them in the most appropriate section.

### 3. Emotional and Social Support
The hardest part of **switching schools in Patna** is leaving old friends. We recommend visiting our campus with your child before their first day. At the **best school in Patna**, our teachers are trained to provide extra emotional care to new joiners.
