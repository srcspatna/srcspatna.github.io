## Why CBSE leads in Competitive Preparation

The alignment between the board syllabus and entrance tests is the biggest of the **benefits of CBSE board for competitive exams**.

### 1. NCERT Alignment
Entrance exams like NEET and JEE Main are almost entirely based on NCERT textbooks. Since these are the standard books for CBSE, students at the **top CBSE school in Patna** have a significant lead over students from other boards.

### 2. Scientific Curriculum Design
One of the major **benefits of CBSE board for competitive exams** is its focus on objective and analytical questioning, which mirrors the format of all major entrance tests in India.

### 3. National Recognition and Consistency
Choosing the CBSE board ensures that the level of education is consistent across the country. This makes it the **best board for students in Patna** who aim for admissions in central universities and premium professional institutes.
