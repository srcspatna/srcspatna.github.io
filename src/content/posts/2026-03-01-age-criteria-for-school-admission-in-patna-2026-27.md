## Summary of Age Criteria for School Admission in Patna

For the 2026-27 session, the age is typically calculated as of **March 31st, 2026**.

### 1. Pre-Primary Levels
- **Nursery:** 3 years+
- **LKG (Lower KG):** 4 years+
- **UKG (Upper KG):** 5 years+
These are the standard **age criteria for pre-school admission in Patna** at SRCS.

### 2. Primary Levels
- **Class 1:** 6 years+
This follows the new National Education Policy (NEP) guidelines, which is a mandatory **age criteria for Class 1 admission in Patna** in all reputable CBSE schools.

### 3. Middle and Secondary
For higher grades, the **age criteria for school admission in Patna** is based on the successful completion of the previous grade and the age mentioned on the Transfer Certificate (TC).
