## Checklist of Documents Required for School Admission in Patna

Keep these documents ready (original and photocopies) before you visit the school.

### 1. Proof of Age
The most important of the **documents required for school admission in Patna** is the Birth Certificate issued by a competent authority (Municipal Corporation/Panchayat). This is mandatory for all grade levels.

### 2. Academic Records
For students seeking **admission in Class 2 and above in Patna**, you must provide:
- Report card/Mark sheet from the previous school.
- Transfer Certificate (TC) countersigned by the appropriate board authority.

### 3. Identity and Residence Proof
As part of the **documents required for school admission in Patna**, you will need:
- Aadhar Card of the student and both parents.
- Passport-sized photographs of the student, father, and mother.

### Submit Your Application Online
You can upload the **documents required for school admission in Patna** directly to the SRCS admission portal. This saves time and ensures a touchless application process. Admissions for the 2026-27 session are open.
