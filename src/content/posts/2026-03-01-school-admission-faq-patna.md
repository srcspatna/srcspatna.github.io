## Common School Admission FAQs in Patna

### 1. When do admissions usually open in Patna?
Most **top schools in Patna** open their registration for the new academic session between October and December. At SRCS, we recommend applying early to secure a seat in your preferred grade.

### 2. What is the minimum age for Class 1 admission?
According to the latest guidelines followed by the **best CBSE schools in Patna**, a child must be 6 years of age as of March 31st of the admission year for entry into Class 1.

### 3. Does the school provide hostel facilities for outstation students?
Yes, as a **premier residential school in Patna**, SRCS offers separate, safe, and modern hostel facilities for boys and girls from Class 3 onwards.

### 4. Is there an entrance test for all classes?
For higher grades, we conduct a friendly academic assessment to understand the child's current level. For pre-primary, it is usually a simple interaction with the child and parents.
