### Fee Structure - Class 1 To 10


| FEE BREAKUP | TYPE | PAYABLE | AMOUNT (₹) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| I TO X |
| Registration Fee | One-time Non-Refundable | At the time of Registration | 3000/- |
| Admission Fee | One-time Non-Refundable | At the time of Admission | 48,000/- |
| Annual Charges | Every Year | At the time of Admission/Every Year | 17,000/- |
| Education Fee | Quarterly Non-Refundable | In Cash / Online / Cheque | 5,900/- |
| Transport Fee | Quarterly Non-Refundable | In Cash / Online / Cheque | 4,400/- |
| Total Payable at the time of Admission | Cash / Online / Cheque | 65,000/- ADMISSION FEE + ANNUAL FEE |
